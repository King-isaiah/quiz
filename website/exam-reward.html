How Our Exam Reward Program Works

A step-by-step guide to earning rewards through education

01

Registration

Create your student account by signing up with your details. This gives you access to the Lumos learning platform.

Sign Up Now
02

Login & Profile Setup

After registration, login to your account and complete your profile. Upload necessary documents for verification.

Login Here
03

Exam Inventory

Browse available exams in our inventory. Each exam has specific subjects, difficulty levels, and reward amounts.

Tip: Start with beginner level exams to build confidence
04

Exam Portal & Instructions

Read all instructions carefully before starting. Each exam has specific rules and time limits.

Important Rules:

  • Time limits are strictly enforced
  • No switching tabs during exam
  • Answer all questions before submitting
  • Review answers before final submission
05

Take the Exam

Click "Start Exam" when ready. The timer begins immediately. Answer questions carefully and use the review feature.

Review & Change Answers
Flag Difficult Questions
Progress Tracker
06

Results & Rewards

View your results immediately after submission. Scores determine reward eligibility. Rewards are processed within 24 hours.

Passing Score: 70% or above
Reward Types: Cash, Scholarships, Certificates
07

View Results & Track Progress

Check your results dashboard for detailed performance analysis. Track your progress across multiple exams.
